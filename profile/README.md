## ðŸ—ºï¸ Our Mission

**AEON Observatory** is a research initiative dedicated to the rigorous auditing of AI ecosystems. We treat the global landscape of AI models not as a static list, but as a dynamic, evolving **biological system**.

By combining **Geometric Topology**, **In-Silico Quasi-Experimental Design (ISQED)**, and **Active Probing**, we provide the mathematical tools to:
* ðŸ›¡ï¸ **Detect Model Cloning:** Identify redundant models and "wrapper" products via **PIER** (Peer-Inexpressible Residual).
* ðŸ§¬ **Map Evolutionary Lineage:** Trace the ancestry of fine-tuned models.
* ðŸ”­ **Reveal Blind Spots:** Use tomographic scanning to find semantic gaps in model capabilities.

## ðŸ”¬ Key Technologies

We build on the intersection of Machine Learning, Convex Optimization, and Distributed Systems.

* **In-Silico Quasi-Experiments:** Creating "parallel universes" to isolate model behavior (Type-A vs Type-B effects).
* **Convex Hull Projection:** Using `cvxpy` to calculate the exact redundancy of neural representations.
* **Active Auditing:** Utilizing Ray for distributed, uncertainty-aware probing of black-box APIs.
